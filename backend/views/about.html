Brief History

The Amazing Racoon Bazaar was an online marketplace platform created by a CMPT276 group of five students AKA Racoons. It all goes back to the fall of 2023 when five students at Simon Fraser University met in the first class of a course CMPT 276. The main idea behind this start up was to provide Sfu students with a unique market place platform especially for educational tools such as books including course books, etc.
